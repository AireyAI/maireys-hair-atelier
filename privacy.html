Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026.

Maireys Hair Market collects only the information needed to respond to enquiries, confirm wig availability, process orders, arrange delivery, and improve the website.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you send by WhatsApp, email, or form.
  • Order preferences such as wig code, texture, length, density, lace type, budget, and delivery area.
  • Optional analytics data if you accept cookies.

How We Use Information

We use information to answer questions, confirm product availability, confirm prices, arrange delivery, and improve the website experience.

Cookies

Analytics cookies are optional. You can accept or decline them from the cookie banner, and you can reopen cookie settings from the footer.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Delivery details may be shared with a courier only when needed to fulfil an order.
