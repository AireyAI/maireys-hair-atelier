# Maireys Hair Market — Design Direction

## Direction: Dark Commerce Marketplace

Inspired by Shopify-style dark commerce and Apple-style product restraint. The site is now a human hair wig marketplace: product cards, product codes, availability, WhatsApp ordering, and delivery confirmation.

## Decision

Proceed with marketplace-first layout because Kyle clarified the business sells human hair wigs only. No treatment, installation, fitting, or salon-service offer should appear on the public site.

## Performance Budget

- LCP < 2s
- CLS < 0.1
- TBT < 200ms
- Lighthouse Performance >= 90
